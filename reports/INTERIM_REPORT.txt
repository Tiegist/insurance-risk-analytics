EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

This interim report summarizes the initial phase of the insurance risk analytics
project, covering exploratory data analysis (EDA) and data version control (DVC)
setup. The analysis is based on historical insurance claim data from February 2014
to August 2015, with the objective of optimizing marketing strategy and discovering
low-risk segments for premium reduction.

Key findings from this phase will be detailed in the final report upon completion
of hypothesis testing and predictive modeling.

   - Linear regression models for each zipcode
   - Claim severity prediction model
   - Premium optimization model

   - Data-backed recommendations for marketing and pricing strategy
